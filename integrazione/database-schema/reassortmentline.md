## ActiveStatus

* 0: Active
* 1: Deactivated
* 2: ActiveOnSFA
* 3: ActiveOnB2B

## GiftQtyMode

* 0: Quantità gratuita 'inclusa' in quella a pagamento
* 1: Quantità gratuita NON 'inclusa' in quella a pagamento

## LineType

* 0: Item
* 1: ManualItem
* 2: Note
* 3: ModelItem
* 4: VariableItem
* 5: Idrolab
* 8: IlDataPool

## LockedFields

* 0: None
* 1: UnitPrice
* 2: Discounts
* 4: SaleType
* 8: PriceList
* 16: QuantitiesAndUom
* 32: Delete
* 64: LineDuplication
